# Product Catalog

## Category Overview

### Beverages
Premium drinks including teas, coffees, and soft drinks. High-margin category with strong repeat purchase rates.

**Key Products**:
- Chai: Traditional spiced tea blend, $18.00
- Chang: Popular carbonated beverage, $19.00

**Seasonality**: Peak sales in summer months (June-August)

### Condiments
Sauces, seasonings, and flavor enhancers. Stable demand year-round.

**Key Products**:
- Aniseed Syrup: Specialty flavoring, $10.00
- Chef Anton's Cajun Seasoning: Premium spice blend, $22.00
- Grandma's Boysenberry Spread: Artisan jam, $25.00
- Northwoods Cranberry Sauce: Seasonal favorite, $40.00

**Seasonality**: Increased demand during holiday season (November-December)

### Dairy Products
Fresh and aged cheeses, milk products. Requires careful inventory management.

**Key Products**:
- Queso Cabrales: Premium Spanish cheese, $21.00

**Shelf Life**: 30-60 days for most products
**Storage**: Refrigeration required

### Meat/Poultry
Premium cuts and specialty meats. High-value category.

**Key Products**:
- Mishi Kobe Niku: Premium Japanese beef, $97.00

**Margin**: 25-35% typical
**Supplier Lead Time**: 5-7 days

### Seafood
Fresh and frozen seafood products. Growing category.

**Key Products**:
- Ikura: Premium salmon roe, $31.00

**Seasonality**: Peak demand during Lent and summer
**Quality Standards**: Grade A required for all products

### Produce
Fresh fruits and vegetables. Organic options available.

**Key Products**:
- Uncle Bob's Organic Dried Pears: $30.00

**Turnover**: High frequency, 2-3 times per week
**Waste Target**: Below 5%

## Pricing Strategy

### Premium Products (>$50)
- Target affluent customers
- Emphasis on quality and exclusivity
- Limited promotions

### Mid-Range Products ($20-$50)
- Mainstream appeal
- Regular promotional opportunities
- Balance of quality and value

### Value Products (<$20)
- High volume potential
- Entry-level products
- Frequent promotions to drive traffic

## Discontinued Products

Products with Discontinued flag set to 1 are:
- No longer available for new orders
- May have remaining stock for existing commitments
- Being phased out due to low sales or supplier issues
