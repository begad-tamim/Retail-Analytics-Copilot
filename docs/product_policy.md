# Product and Business Policies

## Pricing Policies

### Standard Pricing
- All prices listed in USD
- Prices are subject to change with 30 days notice to customers
- Volume discounts available for orders over 100 units of a single product

### Discount Policy
- Maximum discount: 25% off regular price
- Employee discount: 10% on all products
- Seasonal promotions: Up to 40% off during clearance events
- Bulk discount tiers:
  - 50-99 units: 5% discount
  - 100-199 units: 10% discount
  - 200+ units: 15% discount

### Price Matching
- We match competitor prices on identical products
- Price match must be verified before order placement
- Does not apply to clearance or discontinued items

## Inventory Management

### Stock Levels
- Minimum stock level: 10 units per active product
- Reorder point: When stock falls below 15 units
- Maximum stock capacity: Based on warehouse space and turnover rate

### Out of Stock Policy
- Customers notified within 24 hours if ordered item is out of stock
- Option to backorder or substitute with similar product
- Automatic refund if item not available within 14 days

### Discontinued Items
- 90-day notice before discontinuation when possible
- Final orders accepted up to discontinuation date
- No returns accepted after discontinuation date

## Order Policies

### Minimum Order
- Minimum order value: $50
- Minimum quantity per product: 1 unit
- Free shipping on orders over $200

### Order Processing
- Orders placed before 2 PM processed same day
- Orders placed after 2 PM processed next business day
- Processing time: 1-2 business days

### Shipping
- Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
- Express shipping: 2-3 business days (additional $25)
- International shipping: 10-15 business days
- Freight costs based on weight and destination

## Return Policy

### Standard Returns
- 30-day return window from delivery date
- Product must be unopened and in original packaging
- Restocking fee: 15% for opened products

### Perishable Items
- Dairy, Meat/Poultry, Seafood, and Produce: No returns unless defective
- Defective perishables: Report within 48 hours of delivery
- Photo evidence required for perishable claims

### Defective Products
- Full refund or replacement for defective items
- No restocking fee for defective returns
- Prepaid return shipping label provided

## Customer Service

### Business Hours
- Monday-Friday: 8 AM - 6 PM EST
- Saturday: 9 AM - 2 PM EST
- Sunday: Closed

### Response Times
- Email inquiries: Within 24 hours
- Phone calls: Immediate during business hours
- Order status: Real-time tracking available online

### Quality Guarantee
- All products meet or exceed industry quality standards
- Third-party quality certifications maintained
- Quality complaints investigated within 48 hours
